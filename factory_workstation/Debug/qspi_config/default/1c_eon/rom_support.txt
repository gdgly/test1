The following devices have ROM support and do not need MIB keys:


**EN25S80B(2S)**
**EN25S16B(2S)**
EN25S32A(2S)
EN25S64A(2SC)

WARNING: Although the ROM contains support for EN25S16B(2S) and EN25S80B(2S),
         they should not be used because they require a resume to suspend 
         delay of 5ms (according to their datasheets). Revised parts expected
         Q2/Q3 2019.
